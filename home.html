HEAD MESSAGE

Dr.Rajesh Sharma Head of School of Pharmacy

School of Pharmacy of Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore is one of the best institutes in the country and was established on Oct. 15, 2001.The Institute has dedicated young and qualified faculty and is supported by distinguished visiting and guest faculty from industry and professions.It has developed strong linkage with industry which has continuously provided it unstinted support and co-operation. It has a peaceful and congenial environment in a campus, impressive infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories and adequate library facilities.This institute offers a wide array of educational and professional service programmes.The academic programmes, in this School, have been developed to provide educational and personality development facilities for preparing young pharmacists for highly professional and top positions in drug and pharmaceutical industries. If you are an alumnus, we welcome you. Your example and your contributions to the profession serve to inspire our students. It is by your example that future pharmacists learn the meaning of caring, kindness and compassion. It is through your example that future pharmacists learn the importance of leadership. Please keep us informed of your success. You are always welcome. If you are not able to find the information you are seeking or if you find outdated information, please let us know. We are continually improving our site to better serve our community

AIMS & OBJECTIVE

To, provide a specialized high quality education for professional positions in business, industry, academic and government.
To use traditional and modern instructional techniques and technology to the best advantage in the delivery system.
To offer regular educational programmes, only on campus to students.
To provide educational opportunities to persons already employed or engaged in business allowing them to pursue regular degree U.G. & P.G. programmes without any compromise on the quality education.
To pursue innovations and develop new methods and technologies for delivery of education.
To use modern information technology to the maximum extent possible in all phases of the academic as well as administrative aspects of the university programmes.
To offer high quality programmes for training and development of management skills to enhance corporate productivity and efficiency.
To improve the quality, talent and skills of individuals to help them lead their lives as successful professionals and responsible citizen.
To explore the frontiers of knowledge through independent research, as well as in collaboration with other entities in business or education having similar goals.
To aggressively pursue improvements in the quality of higher education through dedication and commitment to teaching, training and research.
To undertake such programmes and activities as are essential to or supportive of the achievement of the above listed objectives.
To help develop industry by extending consultancy services
